Including All Students in FSL : Interactive Web-page - Planning Conversations to Support FSL Students

Introduction

The intent of this interactive site is to build on the approach suggested in the four case studies presented on pages 26 to 29 of "Including Students with Special Education Needs in French as a Second Language Programs". This micro-site provides examples of conversations that school teams may have when planning to support students with special education needs in FSL programs. Note that the strategies suggested in these discussions are a few of the possible options. The purpose of this resource is to provide models of conversations, not an exhaustive list of strategies.

This interactive site is organized into sections highlighting three conversations and various key elements to include in these discussions. Each section has video clips that represent examples of the key element in question. The site also includes a list of the resources mentioned in these conversations. As the strategies suggest, these resources provide a few useful examples and a starting point for further discussions.
